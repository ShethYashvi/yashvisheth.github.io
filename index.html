About Me

Presently, I am Prime Minister’s Research Fellow (most prestigious fellowship awarded to research scholars in India) at IIT Bombay. I am a research enthusiast working in the domain of water treatment under the guidance of Prof. Rajdip Bandyopadhyaya (Department of Chemical Engineering, IIT Bombay) and Prof. Amritanshu Shriwastav (Environmental Science Engineering Department, IIT Bombay). Here, we are trying to solve the emerging issues faced by the presence of heavy metals (a major pollutant in water) in water by developing novel nano composites.

Prior to joining IIT Bombay, I obtained my Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Chemical Engineering from Nirma University (with 8.74 CGPA) and Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University, PDPU (with 9.68 CGPA), respectively.

Apart from research, I am very much fascinated in exploring different places of the world; cooking and trying new food dishes.
